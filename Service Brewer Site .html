Empowering Undocumented Workers. No ID? No Problem.

Who We Are

Service Brewer is a headhunting company built on dignity, grit, and real-world problem-solving. We connect undocumented individuals with paid, legal jobs through biometric verification — bypassing paperwork and opening pathways to real income.

What We Do

We employ individuals without identification by leveraging biometric fingerprinting and IRS-compliant digital payroll. Our workers can earn up to $600 legally without a Social Security number. From city cleanups to service projects, we get boots on the ground and money in their pockets.

We also deduct $1/hour toward helping workers recover official identification — unlocking access to housing, healthcare, and future employment.

Support Our Mission

Your donations go directly to compensating undocumented workers for services rendered — $20/hour, up to 8 hours per day. We’re on a mission to prove that dignity doesn’t need a birth certificate.
